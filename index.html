About Us

Our mission is to be the leading financial services firm in Nigeria through hard work, consistency, and innovative ideas.

BASIL OFILI & CO is a firm of Chartered Accountants duly licensed and registered in Nigeria to practice Accountancy by The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) and the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). The firm was established to provide quality services to large closely-held, medium, and small-scale companies that are usually not accommodated by the big four accountancy firms in the country.
